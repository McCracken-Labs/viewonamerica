Beyond the big outlets sit independent, nonprofit, reader-funded and investigative newsrooms, the kind with more room for critical thinking and adversarial reporting. Sources are grouped by editorial orientation so you can read across viewpoints rather than inside one. Independent media leans left worldwide, so libertarian, conservative and heterodox voices are included on purpose for balance. Heterodox here means outlets that break from the usual left-versus-right script and question the mainstream consensus. The in exile group carries diaspora outlets that report on authoritarian home countries, and on their dealings with Washington, from abroad for safety.
